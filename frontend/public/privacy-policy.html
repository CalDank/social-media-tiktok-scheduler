Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Introduction

Videotto Scheduler ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our TikTok scheduling service (the "Service").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password (hashed), and profile information
  • Social Media Credentials: TikTok access tokens and refresh tokens (stored securely)
  • Content: Videos, captions, titles, and scheduling information for your posts
  • Account Preferences: Platform connections, account settings, and notification preferences

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the Service, including features accessed and actions taken
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, IP address, and operating system
  • Log Data: Server logs, error reports, and performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Provide and Maintain the Service: Process your scheduled posts, manage your account, and deliver the Service
  2. Authenticate Access: Verify your identity and manage your connections to TikTok
  3. Schedule and Post Content: Automatically post your scheduled content to TikTok at the times you specify
  4. Communicate with You: Send you service-related notifications, updates, and support messages
  5. Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality and user experience
  6. Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats

Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: We use industry-standard encryption to protect data in transit and at rest
  • Token Storage: TikTok access tokens are stored securely in encrypted databases
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to user data

Third-Party Services

TikTok Integration

  • OAuth Tokens: We store TikTok OAuth access and refresh tokens to post content on your behalf
  • API Access: We use TikTok's official API to upload videos and publish posts
  • Token Refresh: Access tokens are automatically refreshed when expired
  • Data Sharing: We only share data necessary for posting your scheduled content to TikTok

Your connection to TikTok can be disconnected at any time through the Service settings.

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Scheduled Posts: Retained until deleted by you or posted
  • OAuth Tokens: Retained until you disconnect your TikTok account
  • Log Data: Retained for up to 90 days for security and troubleshooting purposes

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  1. Access: Request access to your personal data
  2. Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  3. Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  4. Disconnect: Disconnect your TikTok account at any time
  5. Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us through the Service or via email.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Maintain your session and authentication state
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze Service usage and performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may limit functionality.
